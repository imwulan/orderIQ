# OrderIQ

> AI Order Assistant that transforms messy WhatsApp conversations into structured orders, business insights, and AI-powered recommendations for food businesses.

---

## Overview

OrderIQ is an AI-powered web application designed to help food businesses manage WhatsApp orders more efficiently.

Instead of manually reading long conversations, OrderIQ extracts important information, tracks order revisions, and generates a structured order summary that is easier for daily operations.

The project was inspired by a real operational problem in my family's restaurant, where customer orders often became difficult to track because conversations changed multiple times before the final order was confirmed.

---

## The Problem

Many small food businesses in Indonesia rely on WhatsApp for receiving orders.

As conversations grow longer, it becomes difficult to identify:

- Final ordered items
- Quantity changes
- Additional menu requests
- Pickup or delivery information
- Final confirmed order

These manual processes frequently lead to operational mistakes and slower service.

---

## The Solution

OrderIQ uses AI to analyze conversations and automatically produce structured order information.

Core capabilities include:

- 📦 Smart Order Extraction
- 🔄 Order Revision Detection
- 🧾 Final Order Summary
- 📊 Order History
- 📈 Business Insights
- 🤖 AI Business Advisor *(Planned)*
- 🖨 Printable Kitchen Order Card *(Planned)*

---

## Why I Built This

Growing up helping my family's restaurant, I experienced these operational problems firsthand.

Instead of building another tutorial project, I wanted to create something that solves a real business problem using AI.

OrderIQ represents how AI can simplify daily business operations for small businesses without changing the tools they already use.
